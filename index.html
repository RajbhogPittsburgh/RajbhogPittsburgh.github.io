Introduction

Welcome to the Rajbhog Pittsburgh Training and Cooking Class References.

This site was developed to give you a quick reference site for all training delivered at Rajbhog Pittsburgh. We hope that you enjooy the information and find it useful. However, if there is more you would like to contribute, you can! Read below how.

What you will find on this site

After watching YouTube, or attending one of our on-site training sessions (cooking classes, for example), come here for:

  • Recipes
  • Health benefits
  • Additional reading references
  • Material mentioned in the class but not recorded on YouTube
  • Recipes used in the cooking classes

Structure of this site

Very simple! Under the logo, you will find a "breadcrumb" style menu starting with "HOME" and with individual Seaons. Each episode is cataloged by the season in which it appears.

For example, S1 E2 means Season 1, Episode 2. Click on "Season 1" to bring up that page.

When you have the right season, you can then select the Episode either by scrolling down the page or clicking on the Episode number on the left hand navigation panel.

How you can contribute

If you have additional feedback, ideas, references, or information you believe would be beneficial for others, we would be happy to include it. You can either put that in the comments of the training video, or email us directly at:

Email: info@RajbhogPittsburgh.com

and we will review to include the information that is appropriate.

Have fun cooking and experimenting!
